Download Gerber ZIP

Note

Make sure both the transmitter and receiver have the same channel and data rate settings. The nRF24L01 modules require a stable 3.3V supply — use a decoupling capacitor (10µF–100µF) near the module's VCC pin to avoid communication dropouts.
